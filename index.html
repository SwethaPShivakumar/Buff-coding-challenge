Our Founder

In 2019, Emma took a plunge to create something unique, leaving behind a successful career in the retail and beauty industry and taking with her 15 years of insight into what makes an exceptional customer experience she plunged into the wild west of what is the world of nail salons in Australia.

Appalled by the fact the industry is largely unregulated and by how low-grade high street salons had driven prices down in turn breeding a nasty underbelly of underpaid, overworked and (largely) unqualified nail technicians servicing the women of Melbourne.

Many other beauty experiences have been transformed over the years, nails had been left out in the cold. She believed that everyone deserves to enjoy expert nail care service in a clean, modern, luxurious space. Somewhere that specialised in their craft and sat between a high end beauty salon and the cheap and cheerful nail salons where prices are ridiculously cheap, hygiene is minimal and customer service seems to not be a consideration.
